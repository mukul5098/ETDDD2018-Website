What are the accommodation charges for student?

An amount of Rs.1000/- per student is accommodation fee for all three days subject to following conditions:
1. All the items provided are returned in proper condition.
2. No damage has been done to the room where accommodation has been provided

Will I have to pay only half the amount if I stay only for one day?

Even if you stay for one day you will be charged the same amount.

Is accommodation based on first come first serve basis?

Yes, a limited number of accommodation will be available in hostel/dormitory on first come first serve basis.

Is there any deadline for registering for accommodation?

It is based on first come first serve basis and the registrations will be accomplished as soon as the dead line gets past its stipulated value.

How do I reach IIT (BHU) Varanasi from railway station?

There are taxis and auto-rickshaws available at nominal charges at railway station, which will bring you at the campus gates. Our volunteers at the gates will guide you to reach the IIT, which is at the other end of the campus.

Is there any internal transport considering the huge area (1300 acres) of your campus?

Internal transport will be available at reasonable rates. There is free Bus facility and also auto rickshaws are usually available in campus.

Are there ATMs inside the campus?

There are SBI and Bank of Baroda ATMs inside the campus. There is also an ICICI and HDFC ATM just outside the campus.

Are there sufficient medical facilities inside the campus?

Every hostel where you get accommodation will have a first-aid kit. There is a very large Sir SunderLal Hospital inside the BHU campus.

Where should I collect my prizes and certificates?

All prizes and certificates are to be given during valedictory function. Details about the same will be given to you when you register for an event.
