Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology, IIT(BHU), Varanasi

Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering & Technology, Indian Institute of Technology(BHU) is pioneer in pharmaceutical education in India at university level. Prof. Mahadev Lal Schroff initiated this in July 1932 under the inspiring guidance of Mahamana Madan Mohan Malaviya. This department has provided pharmacists, researchers and academicians who are leaders in their respective field throughout the globe.



Distance from Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport (VNS): Approx. 30 km, Distance from Varanasi Junction Railway Station: Approx. 10 km
